Why are they called new jeans?

Newjeans are called newjeans due to the idea that jeans are seen as a timeless piece of fashion. Ever since 1873 jeans have a chokehold on our fashion and shaped the way we've percieved it.

The name is also a word play on the phrase "new genes", referring to the group creating a new generation of pop music.
